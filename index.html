How to use Quick Track:

Set Default Meds is where you will set your default medication cocktail and usual pain level. This information will be recorded when using instant log.


Instant Log allows you to instantly log the time and date of your migraine episode with a single tap. Once your episode is over, you can add fields like "Pain", "Aura duration", and more.


Precision Log allows you to log your migraine episode in great detail, including which medications you take, aura symptoms, and more.


History is where you'll view all of your episodes. From this page, you can view, edit or favorite your migraine episodes for later.
